11. Street market

What is the difference between street markets and supermarkets?

Do you want to go to the street markets in foreign countries?
Have you ever been to street markets in other countries?
Is it different from the street markets in china?
Which one do you prefer, outdoor markets, supermarkets or shopping mall?
Which one do you prefer, street markets or supermarkets, why?
Do you like go to the supermarkets?
Why do people like going to the supermarket?
What do street markets sell?

12. Outdoor activities

Did you often go over to your friends’ house when you were young?
Is it important for children to play outdoors?

13. Spare time with family

What do you usually do when you are free?

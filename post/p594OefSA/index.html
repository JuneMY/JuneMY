#雅思# #口语# 常用原因&解释 -- 开心

2019-12-29 / 9 min read

雅思口语中，我们经常会需要表达自己的情绪，开心，但是我们不能一直使用happy，那么积累一些不同的近义词就极为重要。除了掌握单词的样子，还要学会发音和正确用法。

小烤鸭们当然不需要记这么多！只需要从中选出4-5个自己看着喜欢的，也能够发音发正确的即可。

A

amused
英文解释：
例子：
She was very amused by/at your comments.
**Toddlers don't need expensive toys and games to keep them amused. **

B

beaming
英文解释：
used to describe a smile that is very wide and happy, or someone who is smiling in this way
例子：
a beaming smile

blissful
英文解释：
extremely or completely happy
例子：
We spent a blissful year together before things started to go wrong.
a blissful childhood/holiday
Everyone loves the blissful feeling of going to sleep.
We spent a blissful evening at the beach.

blithe
英文解释：
happy and without worry
例子：
She shows a blithe disregard for danger.

buoyant
英文解释：
happy and confident
例子：
**After reading the letter he was in a buoyant mood. **

C

carefree
英文解释：
having no problems or not being worried about anything
例子：
I remember my carefree student days.

cheerful
英文解释：
(1) happy and positive
例子：
He's usually fairly cheerful.
You're in a cheerful mood this morning.
(2) a place or thing that makes you feel positive and happy
例子：
Turn that dreadful wailing music off and put on something cheerful.

cheery
英文解释
bright and happy
例子：
She walked in with a cheery "Good morning!"
He gave us a cheery wave as we drove past.

chipper
英文解释
very happy
例子：
You seem mighty chipper this morning. what's up?
*The dog seems very chipper. he won't stop playing. *
He's in a chipper mood because he has a date tonight.

chirpy
英文解释
happy and active
例子：
**She seemed quite chirpy this morning. **

content
英文解释
pleased with your situation and not hoping for change or improvement
例子：
**He seems fairly content with his life. **

contented
英文解释
happy and satisfied
例子：
She smiled a contented smile.

D

delighted
英文解释
very pleased
例子：
**Pat was delighted with her new house. **

E

ebullient
英文解释
very energetic, positive , and happy
例子：
He wasn't his usual ebullient self.

ecstatic
例子：
He was ecstatic when his wife gave birth to their first child.
Wes was ecstatic to see an FC Barcelona match.
We were ecstatic when we heard news of his promotion.

elated
英文解释
excited and happy combined
例子：
**The mother was elated when she watched her daughter's performance. **
He was elated after winning the context.

enraptured
英文解释
filled with great pleasure or extremely pleased by something
例子：
**The audience was enraptured by the young soloist's performance. **

euphoric
英文解释
a state of extreme happiness and joy
例子：
The dog felt euphoric as he ran into the water.
**I watched an amazing sunrise and i felt euphoric. **

exhilarated
英文解释
very excited and happy
例子：
At the end of the race i was exhilarated.

exultant
英文解释
very happy, especially at someone else's defeat or failure
例子：
an exultant cheer

G

glad
英文解释
pleased and delighted
例子：
I am glad you came to the party.
I'll be glad to write you a letter of recommendation.

gleeful
英文解释
happy, excited, or pleased
例子：
a gleeful smile/shout
I rubbed my hands in gleeful anticipation before settling down to sleep.

I

in a good mood
英文解释
feeling happy or pleasant
例子：
**People were in a good mood because progress was being made. **

in good spirits
英文解释
feeling happy or cheerful
例子：
He seemed sad the last time i saw him, i'm glad he's in good spirits tonight.

***in seventh heaven ***
英文解释
extremely happy
例子：
Since they got married, they’re in seventh heaven .

invigorated
英文解释
to make someone feel fresher, healthier, and more energetic:
例子：
We were invigorated by our walk.

J

jaunty
英文解释
showing that you are happy and confident
例子：
a jaunty grin/step

jocular
英文解释
used to describe someone who is happy and likes to make jokes
例子：
Michael was in a very jocular mood at the party.

jolly
英文解释
happy and cheerful
例子：
Santa Claus is a jelly fellow.
**My father is a jolly man. He's always laughing and joking. **

jovial
英文解释
(of a person) friendly and in a good mood, or (of a situation) enjoyable because of being friendly and pleasant:
例子：
He seemed a very jovial guy.
a jovial time/evening/chat

joyful
英文解释
very happy
例子：
The child's joyful laugh made us smile.
**I had a joyful reaction when i saw the engagement ring. **

joyous
英文解释
full of joy; very happy
例子：
a joyous hymn/event/voice

jubilant
英文解释
feeling or expressing great happiness, especially because of a success
例子：
The fans were jubilant at/about/over their team's victory.

L

lighthearted
英文解释：
happy and not serious
例子：
**It was a fairly light-hearted discussion. **

lively
英文解释
full of life and energy
例子：
The small gathering of people turned into a lively party.

M

merry
英文解释
feeling or expressing great happiness, especially because of a success
例子：
The fans were jubilant at/about/over their team's victory.

mirthful
英文解释
full of laughter, humour, or happiness:
例子：
The ending of the film is melancholy rather than mirthful.

O

on cloud nine
英文解释
to be extremely happy and excited:
例子：
*When XX came home with a rescue kitten. XX was on cloud nine. *
He was on cloud nine after passing his drivers license test.

on top of the world
英文解释
extremely happy
例子：
We finally bought the house of our dreams, i'm on top of the world.

over-the-moon
英文解释
to be very pleased
例子：
I can't believe i got the job! i'm over the moon.
*She was over the moon about/with her new bike. *
She was over the moon when she found out she was pregnant.
He was over the moon when he finished graduate school.

overjoyed
英文解释
extremely happy
例子：
I'm overjoyed that i've been accepted into university.
We're overjoyed at your news.

P

pleased
英文解释
happy or satisfied
例子：
I'm pleased to hear you're feeling better.
You could tell from her voice that she wasn't pleased.

R

radiant
英文解释
obviously very happy, or very beautiful
例子：
He gave a radiant smile when he heard her news.
The bride looked radiant on her wedding day.

rapturous
英文解释
showing extreme pleasure and happiness or excitement:
例子：
The play was greeted with rapturous applause.
The team received a rapturous welcome.

S

satisfied
英文解释
pleased because you have got what you wanted, or because something has happened in the way that you wanted
例子：
A satisfied smile
Are you satisfied with the new arrangement?

sunny
英文解释
a sunny person is usually happy and relaxed and does not usually get worried or angry
例子：
**She has a very sunny disposition. **

T

thrilled
英文解释
extremely happy about something
例子：
She was thrilled when she heard the good news.
I was thrilled that so many people turned up to the party.
He's thrilled to go skydiving for the first time.

tickled pink
英文解释 imfml
very pleased
例子：
**I was tickled pink to be invited. **

U

upbeat
英文解释：
full of hope, happiness, and good feeling
例子：
Live music and a parade set an upbeat mood for the official opening.
The song was really upbeat and put us in a good mood.
Even though the flight got cancelled, she remains upbeat about the situation.

W

walking on air
英文解释：
to be very happy and excited because something very good has happened to you
例子：
*Ever since she met Mark, she's been walking on air. *
They were walking on air after winning the match.
She was walking on air when she finished her exams.

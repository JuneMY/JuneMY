#雅思# 一个完整的interview

2019-11-29 / 2 min read

对于一个新烤鸭来说，了解考试流程尤为重要，那么上面的音频中的就是一次完整的雅思口语考试。
下面的是问题，大家可以试着自己练习一下，然后跟录音中的进行一些比较额。
PS 录音中非9分范文，仅供考参考一下考试形式。

问题：

Part1

Can you tell me your full name please?
Can I see your identification please?

Do you live in a house or an apartment?
Is there anything you want to change about your neighborhood?

Clothes

What do you like to wear when you are at home?
What do people in your country like to wear to parties?
Do you like to try different kind of fashion？
Do people in your country usually like to wear formal or casual clothes？

Politeness

Is it important to be polite in your country?
How do children learn good manners？
Who do you think is usually more polite, older or younger people?
Do you think more people are more polite at home with family or with people they don't know?

Part 2

Describe a difficult decision that you made.
You should say:
what decision it was
what difficulties you faced
how you made the decision

Have you told anyone else about this?

Part 3

What are some decisions that most people need to make these days?
Who do you think usually give the best advice in your culture?
What disadvantages are there when other people give you advice when you have to make a decision?

What are the important things that a teenager has to make decisions about?
How can your age make a difference to the way you approach decision making?

the end~

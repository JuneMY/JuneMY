# 雅思# 2020年1-4月雅思口语话题part 1

2020-01-05 / 10 min read

注：非原创，广大试水烤鸭们的成果，有需要的自取即可。
加油！～ 最可爱的烤鸭们～～

新题

Art

Do you like art?
Did you have art classes when you were at school?
Have you ever visited an art gallery?
Do you often visit art galleries?
Is there any artwork on the wall in your room?

Break time

Do you prefer to work/study in the morning or afternoon?
Do you prefer short breaks or long breaks?
What do you do during breaks?
Why do people need to take breaks?

Being alone

Do you like to be alone?
What was the last time when you were alone and what did you do then?
What do you always do when you are alone?

Dessert

Do you like dessert?
Do you like eating cakes?
Have you ever made cake?
Special cake in your country?

History

Do you like history?
When you were a child, did you enjoy learning history?
Have you ever been to a museum to learn about history?
Have you ever watched movies related to history?
When was the last time you read a book about history?
Do you think history is important?

Laugh

Do you like to watch movies or TV shows that make people laugh?
Are you the kind of person who makes people laugh?
Do you usually make your friends laugh?
When was the last time you laughed? (Why?)
Do you like to make people laugh?
Do you like watching funny TV shows?
Do you think it's important for friends to have the same sense of humour?
Do you think it's important for friends to laugh together?

Number

Are you good at remembering numbers?
Do you often use numbers to do something?
Is there any special number you like?
Do you have a favourite number?
Are you good at numbers (maths)?
Would you like to have a job dealing with numbers?

Parks

Are there any parks/public facilities near where you live?
Do people in your area like these parks?
What do you do when you go to a park?
Do people in your country like going to parks?

Pen or pencil

Do you usually use pen or pencil?
Which do you use more often? Pen or pencil?
When was the last time you bought a pen or pencil?
What do you think if someone gives you a pen or a pencil as a present?
How would you feel if someone gave you a pen as s gift?
Did your teachers teach you how to write with a pencil or pen?

Science

Do you like science?
Did you have science classes when you were in primary or middle school?
Do you think these classes are helpful in your adult life?
Do you read science magazines?
Is science important?

Scenery/view

Is there good scenery in your hometown?
When you travel, do you like to live in hotels with scenic views?
Do you like to stay in places with beautiful views when you are traveling?
Do you like to take pictures of good scenery with your smartphone? Why?
Would you say people enjoy taking pictures of beautiful scenery?
Can you suggest why some people use their mobile phone to take pictures of beautiful views?

Running

Do you like running?
Where do you usually go running?
How often do you go for a run?
Have you ever run a long distance?
Do schools organise running activities?
Have you watched any running competitions?
When was the last time you take for a long run, why?

Water sports

Are water sports popular in China?
What’s the most popular water sport in China?
Do you like playing in the water?
Have you done water sports?
Are there any water sports you would like to try in the future?
What water sports do you like doing?

老题

Work/study

Why did you choose to do that type of work (or, that job）？
Do you miss being a student？
Is it very interesting？
Do you like your job？
Why did you choose to study that subject? / Why did you choose to study those subjects？
Is it very interesting？
What subject (s) are you studying？
Do you prefer to study in the mornings or the afternoons？
Are you looking forward to working？
Do you like your subject? (why/ why not？）

The area you live in

Do you like the area that you live in?
What are some changes in the area recently?
Do you know any famous people in your area?
Where do you like to go in that area?

Your accommodation

What kind of housing/ accommodation do you live in？
Do you live in a house or a flat？
What's the difference between where you are living now and where you have lived in the past？
Can you describe the place where you live？
Which room does your family spend most of the time in？
How long have you lived there？
What do you usually do in your house/fat/room?
Do you plan to live there for a long time？
Are the transport facilities to your home very good？
Please describe the room you live in？
Do you prefer living in a house or a flat？
What part of your home do you like the most？

Countryside

Do you enjoy living in the countryside?
Will you live in the countryside in the future?
What do you usually do in the countryside?
Have you ever lived in the countryside?

Concentration

What might distract you when you’re trying to stay focused?
When do you need to be focused?
Is it difficult for you to stay focused on sth?
What do you do to help you concentrate?

Friends

What kinds of people do you like to make friends with?
What do you usually do when you are with your friends?
Do you keep in contact with any of your friends from your childhood?
What do you think makes a good friend?
Would you say you are a good friend to others?
Do you prefer to spend time with your family or with your friends?
Which do you think is more important, your family or your friends?

Haircut

When was the last time you got a haircut?
What hairstyle did you go for?
How often do you change your hairstyle?
Were you happy about the last haircut you got?

Jeans

How often do you wear jeans?
Do you like wearing jeans?
Why do you think jeans are popular?
Do you think jeans will be more popular in the future?

Languages

How many languages do you speak?
Are you currently learning other languages?
How did you learn the language you speak?
Do you think English is hard to learn?

Music

When do you listen to music?
What's your favourite type of music?
Do you prefer to listen to music alone, or with others?
Has your preference in music changed since you were younger?
How much time do you spend listening to music every day?
What kinds of music do you like to listen to?
Have you ever been to a concert before?

Movie

What is your favourite movie?
Do your friends like this movie?
What kinds of movies do you think young people like?
Do you want to be a movie star?

Patience

What do you think patience is?
Do you think patience is important?
Do you think you are a patient person?
Have you ever lost your patience?
Were you patient when you were young?
How do you feel when other people are not patient?
Were you less or more patient when you were angry?

Photos

Do you like to take photos?
Do you prefer to take photos yourself or to have other people take photos?
How often do you take photos?
In what situations do you take photos?
How do you keep your photos?

Public holiday

How many public holidays do you have in China?
Do you think people need more public holidays?
HOW do you usually spend your holidays?
Which holiday is your favourite?

Reading

Do you like to read?
What kind of books do you read?
Do you read books about what you are studying?
Do you read more often for study or for fun?
Do you prefer reading e-books or paper books?

Travel

Do you like travelling?
Which cities have you travelled to?
What kind of cities do you like to travel to?
What is the place that left you the deepest impression when travelling?

Tea or coffee

Do you often drink coffee or tea?
When was the last time you drank tea or coffee?
What do you usually prepare for your guests, tea or coffee?
Do people in your country give others coffee or tea as a gift?
Do Chinese people like to drink tea or coffee?

Voices

Has your voice ever changed?
Do you enjoy recording your voice and listening to it?
Do you like your own voice?
Does your voice sound familiar to your parents'?

Walking

Do you walk a lot?
Where do you usually take a walk?
Do you think people will walk more or not in the future?
Do you walk more often than in the past?

Wild animals

Have you ever kept pets?
Do you like to go to the zoo?
Have you ever seen any wild animals before?
What wild animal do you like most?

2019年9-12月雅思口语话题

2019-11-20 4 min read # 雅思口语 # 雅思 # 口语
  1. Amusement park

Do you like going to amusement parks?
How often do you go to amusement parks?
What do you usually do at amusement parks?
Do you think facilities in amusement parks are safe to get on?

  1. Makeup

Do you often wear makeup?
What does wear makeup mean to you?
Would you give makeup as gifts?
How do you feel about man wearing makeup?

  1. Fatigue/tiredness

What makes you feel tired?
What do you do when you are tired?
Who do you prefer to talk to about your worries, friends or families?
Do you want to talk to others when you are tired?

  1. Eating

How often do you eat with your family members?
Do you enjoy having healthy foods?
Do you and your family eat out often?
Do you prefer eating out or cook at home?

  1. Weather (sunshine)

Do you enjoy sunshine?
What do you usually do on sunny days?
Have you ever used sunscreen?
Will you enjoy living in a place where the weather is different?

  1. Outdoor activity (forest)

Do you prefer indoor or outdoor activities?
What do people like to do outdoors?
Do you like walking in a forest when you are free?
When was the last time you went to a forest?

  1. Hair cut

When was the last time you got a haircut?
What hairstyle did you go for?
How often do you change your hairstyle?
Were you happy about the last haircut you got?

  1. Jeans

How often do you wear jeans?
Do you like wearing jeans?
Why do you think jeans are popular?
Do you think jeans will be more popular in the future?

  1. Visiting hometown

How often do you go back to your hometown?
How often do you visit your grandparents?
What do you bring as gifts when you go back for visits?
Do you enjoy visiting friends more or families more?

10． Vacation

How often do you go on vacation?
Do you want a long or short vacation?
What was the most exciting thing you experienced in a trip?
What is your dream vacation like?

  1. Languages

How many languages do you speak?
Do you think it’s difficult to learn a new language?
Why do you learn English?
Are you currently learning other languages?
How did you learn the language you speak?
Do you think English is hard to learn?

  1. Social media

Do you use the social media often?
What do you use the social media for?
Do you think making new friends on social media is a good thing?
Do you think you will use social media more or less frequently?

  1. Island

Have you ever been on an island?
Are there islands in your country?
Do you want to live on an island?
What would you like to do while you are on an island?

  1. Voice

Has your voice ever changed?
Do you like your own voice?
Do you enjoy recording your voice and listening to it?
Does your voice sound similar to your parents’?

  1. Walk

Do you walk a lot?
Do you walk more often than in the past?
Do you think people will walk more or not in the future?
Where do you usually take a walk?
